About the show

Where experience
becomes insight

The luxury jewelry industry runs on expertise built over decades — and most of it never gets written down. Carats & Connections changes that. Each episode goes deep with the founders, designers, and specialists who have spent careers mastering their craft, their market, and their clientele.

No surface-level success stories. No rehearsed talking points. Just honest, high-level conversation about what it actually takes to build and sustain a luxury brand in today's market.
